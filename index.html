Frequently Asked Questions

How does RAGOps Studio detect hallucinations?
Our system compares generated answers against source documents using multiple verification methods including claim decomposition, entailment checking, and source attribution scoring.
Can I deploy on-premise?
Yes, Enterprise plans include on-premise deployment options with full air-gap support for sensitive environments.
What embedding models do you support?
We support OpenAI, Cohere, and HuggingFace models out-of-the-box. Custom embeddings can be integrated via our Python SDK.
How is my data secured?
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're SOC 2 Type II compliant and offer data residency controls for Enterprise customers.
